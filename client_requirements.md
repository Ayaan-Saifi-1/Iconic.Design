# Iconic Kitchen - Client Requirements Checklist

To successfully launch the Iconic Kitchen website, we need the following assets, information, and configurations from the client. 

## 1. Branding & Assets
- [ ] **Logo:** High-resolution logo in SVG or transparent PNG format (preferably a dark version for light backgrounds and a light version for the dark navbar).
- [ ] **Favicon:** Small icon for the browser tab (usually 32x32 or 16x16 PNG/ICO).
- [ ] **Brand Colors:** Exact color codes (Hex or RGB) if they prefer something specific. (The current template uses Navy `#0f1e3c` and Gold `#d4af37`. Let us know if we should stick with this premium look).
- [ ] **Typography/Fonts:** Specific fonts if they have brand guidelines (currently using Poppins and Playfair Display).

## 2. Imagery & Media
- [ ] **Hero Image/Video:** The main, high-quality background image or short looping video for the homepage banner. This is the first thing users will see.
- [ ] **About Section Image:** A photo representing the company (e.g., the team, a showroom, or a stunning kitchen).
- [ ] **Project/Portfolio Images:** 
  - High-quality cover photos for their featured past projects.
  - Gallery images for each specific project's detail page (up to 5-6 images per project).
- [ ] **Service Icons/Images:** If they prefer specific images over standard Font Awesome icons for their services (e.g., Custom Cabinetry, Countertops).

## 3. Copy & Content (Text)
- [ ] **Tagline/Headline:** The main catchy text for the homepage hero section (e.g., "Crafting Your Dream Kitchen").
- [ ] **About Us Text:** A paragraph or two about their history, mission, and expertise.
- [ ] **Services List:** A definitive list of the services they offer (e.g., Modular Kitchens, Wardrobes, Complete Renovations) with a short 1-2 sentence description for each.
- [ ] **Key Statistics:** Numbers for the homepage (e.g., "X+ Kitchens Delivered", "X Years Experience", "X Expert Designers").
- [ ] **Testimonials:** 3 to 4 real client reviews (including the client's name and a short quote).
- [ ] **Initial Portfolio Data:** Details for a few key projects to showcase on launch:
  - Project Title
  - Location
  - Scope of work
  - Completion Date

## 4. Contact & Business Details
- [ ] **Business Address:** Full physical address for the footer.
- [ ] **Contact Phone Number(s):** For the header, footer, and contact page.
- [ ] **Inquiry Email Address:** The email address where they want to receive leads from the website's contact form.
- [ ] **Social Media Links:** URLs for their Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, LinkedIn, etc.

## 5. Technical & Deployment (Backend)
- [ ] **Domain Name:** Do they already own a domain (e.g., `iconickitchen.com`), or do they need us to purchase and configure one?
- [ ] **Admin Password:** A secure password they want to use to log into the website's admin dashboard to add/edit projects.
- [ ] **SMTP Credentials:** For the contact form to actually send emails to their inbox, we will need the email credentials (e.g., a Gmail App Password or professional email SMTP settings).
- [ ] **Legal Pages (Optional but recommended):** Privacy Policy and Terms of Service text.
