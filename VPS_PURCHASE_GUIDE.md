# 🖥️ Guide to Purchasing a VPS for Iconic Design

A VPS (Virtual Private Server) is where your website's files and code will live. It runs 24/7 so your clients can visit "Iconic Design" at any time.

Here is everything you need to know to purchase the right VPS for your interior design firm.

---

## 1. What Specifications Do You Need?
Your website is built with Python (Flask) and standard HTML/CSS/JS. It is lightweight and highly optimized. You do **not** need an expensive, heavy-duty server. 

**Recommended Starting Specs (Hostinger's KVM 1 Plan):**
*   **OS (Operating System):** Ubuntu 22.04 LTS or 24.04 LTS (This is the industry standard and easiest to work with).
*   **CPU:** 1 vCPU Core
*   **RAM:** 4 GB RAM (This is an excellent amount of memory and will ensure your site runs incredibly smoothly).
*   **Storage:** 50 GB NVMe Disk Space (Extremely fast storage with more than enough room for your high-quality portfolio images).
*   **Type:** "Unmanaged" Linux VPS with Full Root Access (This means you manage the software, giving you full control).
*   **Other included features:** Dedicated IP Address, Snapshots & Backups (Great for security and peace of mind).

## 2. Recommended Providers (Beware of Renewal Pricing!)

**1. DigitalOcean (Best for Flat, Cheap Pricing)**
*   **Why it's great:** They charge a flat rate of **$4 per month (~₹350)** for a 1GB RAM server. **The price never doubles when you renew.** It stays $4 forever.
*   **Payment:** They charge in USD, so you need to use a Debit/Credit card with international transactions enabled, or PayPal. 
*   **Verdict:** If you want to avoid price traps, this is the best option.

**2. Hostinger India (Best for GPay, but watch the renewal)**
*   **Why it's great:** They explicitly accept **UPI and Google Pay**. They give you huge specs (4GB RAM) for the first term.
*   **The Catch:** While your first year might be cheap (around ₹400/mo), the **renewal price jumps to ₹999/mo**.
*   **Verdict:** Great if you strictly need to pay with GPay right now, but it is more expensive in the long run.

**3. MilesWeb / BigRock (Local Indian Alternatives)**
*   **Why it's great:** Indian companies that support UPI/GPay and often have cheaper long-term unmanaged VPS plans without the massive renewal jump.

## 3. Recommended Duration
*   **Option 1: 1 Month (For Testing)** - If you just want to set it up, see how it looks, and make sure everything is perfect, pay for just 1 month. You can always renew later.
*   **Option 2: 1 Year (For the Discount)** - Providers like Hostinger offer massive discounts (often 50% off) if you commit to 12 months upfront. If you are confident in your business, this saves you a lot of money in the long run.

---

## 4. Step-by-Step Purchase Process (Example: Hostinger)

1.  **Go to the Website:** Visit [Hostinger.in](https://www.hostinger.in) (or your chosen provider) and navigate to the **VPS Hosting** section.
2.  **Select the Plan:** Choose the plan that matches the specs above (usually called "KVM 1" or "VPS 1").
3.  **Choose Duration:** Select 1 month or 12 months.
4.  **Create an Account:** Sign up with your email or Google account.
5.  **Select Payment Method:** Choose **UPI / Google Pay** from the list of payment options.
6.  **Complete Payment:** Scan the QR code with your GPay app or enter your UPI ID to complete the transaction.

## 5. What Happens After You Pay?
Once payment is successful, the provider will ask you a few setup questions:
*   **Server Location:** Choose a location closest to your target clients (e.g., **Mumbai** or **Pune** if your clients are in India. This makes the website load faster for them).
*   **Operating System:** Select **Ubuntu 22.04 LTS** or **Ubuntu 24.04 LTS** (Plain OS, no control panel needed).
*   **Create a Password:** You will be asked to create a "Root Password". **Save this password securely.** You will need it to log into the server.

## 🎉 What's Next?
Once your VPS is active, the provider will give you an **IP Address** (e.g., `192.168.1.50`). 

When you reach this point, simply give me that IP Address and your Root Password, and I will connect to the server, install all the necessary software, and put your website live on the internet!
